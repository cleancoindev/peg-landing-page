PEG TOKEN

PEG serves as a universal collateral token used to issue and stabilize asset-pegged tokens. Any user can deposit PEG as a collateral into a Vault, and receive custom tokens pegged to a real-world currency, commodity, or underlying asset of their choice.

PEG Tokens have instant liquidity and can be convered to any ERC20 token via Bancor’s Liquidity Network.

PEG INSTANCE MANAGERS

WHO CAN BE A PEG INSTANCE MANAGER?

A PEG Instance is the management layer responsible for setting the terms for creating and maintaining an asset-pegged token. The PEG Instance Manager will be responsible for setting price thresholds, establishing terms of debt and reporting updated asset price.

VAULT CREATORS

WHY BE A VAULT CREATOR?

A Vault is a user-ownrd smart contract cillateralized by PEG Network. User can mint asset-pagged tokens by despositing collateral into a Vault which is tied to a parent PEG istance.

TRADER

Instantly trade aset-pagged tokens on PEGDEX allowing for participation in previosly inaccessibe derivative markets.

PEG FOUNDATION

PEG Foundation is a Swiss Non Profit regulated under the Swiss Financial Authority (FINMA) and based in Zurich, Switzerland with an R&D Center in City of Hayden, Idaho, USA.

The PEG Foundation is dedicated to the development and promotion of the PEG Protocol, a blockchain agnostic standart for creation of non volatile, synthethic cryptographic asset, commonly referred to as Stable Coins.
